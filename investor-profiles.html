001

Conservative
Investor

Conservative investors prioritize capital preservation and stable returns. They typically allocate a significant portion of their portfolio to established assets with proven track records.

In the crypto space, conservative investors often focus on major cryptocurrencies with high market capitalization and established infrastructure.

002

Moderate
Investor

Moderate investors seek a balanced approach between risk and return. They maintain a diversified portfolio that includes both established assets and promising emerging opportunities.

This profile often includes a mix of large-cap cryptocurrencies and carefully selected mid-cap projects with strong fundamentals.

003

Aggressive
Investor

Aggressive investors are willing to take higher risks in pursuit of potentially higher returns. They actively seek out emerging opportunities and are comfortable with market volatility.

In crypto, aggressive investors often explore early-stage projects, DeFi protocols, and innovative blockchain solutions with high growth potential.
